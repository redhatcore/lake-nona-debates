About the Club

Lake Nona Debates is completely free to join and open to the public. We meet once a week in Lake Nona, Florida. Each meeting runs 30 minutes. A main idea is introduced and debated by two teams of three — one Affirmative, one Negative — followed by a short wrap-up. It’s a fast, engaging way to develop your communication and critical thinking skills.
